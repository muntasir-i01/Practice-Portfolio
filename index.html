Model Makers at Tech 3D have been producing world class models for over three decades. Our talented staff work with the customer from start to finish during the design and build process. Tech 3D Prints Private Limited is one of the pioneers in the architectural model making industry in Oman. Setup in 2010, the company has unique value systems that have created an unparalleled name with the Real Estate Developers, Engineers and Architects.

We specialize in producing commercial and residential architectural models, industrial models, exhibition models, mechanical and heavy engineering models, R&D models, prototype models and sculpture models. Tech 3D models have assisted several architects and project developers to stand out from the growing competition by showcasing their designs and ideas in a visually appealing way.

Our architectural 3D modeling can be used for large scale model making, a single building, massing/spatial/detailed models or shadow studies. Geographic Information Systems 3D data can be printed to show actual city landscapes, and proposed buildings can be added to show the effect on a city’s skyline.
